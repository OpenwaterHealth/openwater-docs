# Governance

Openwater is governed by a Technical Steering Committee (TSC).

## Technical Steering Committee

### Composition

- 3 Openwater representatives
- 3 academic representatives
- 3 community-elected members

### Responsibilities

- Strategic technical direction
- Approve major architectural changes
- Manage RFC process
- Community guidelines and policies

## Decision Making

Major decisions follow the RFC (Request for Comments) process:

1. Submit RFC document
2. 2-week community comment period
3. TSC review and discussion
4. Simple majority vote for approval

## Elections

Community representatives elected annually by contributors with 5+ merged PRs.
