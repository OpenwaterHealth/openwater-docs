# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in Openwater a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviors:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards community members

**Unacceptable behaviors:**
- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Trolling or insulting/derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to community@openwater.health. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
