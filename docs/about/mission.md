# Mission & Vision

## Our Mission

Democratize medical device innovation through open-source collaboration, reducing development costs from $119M to $15M and serving 3.5 billion underserved people globally.

## Our Vision

Build the "Linux of Medical Devices" - an ecosystem where global innovators collaborate to create accessible, high-quality medical technologies.

## The Problem

- **$119M** average device development cost
- **72%** market control by 3 companies
- **3.5B** people without basic medical imaging
- **7 years** typical time to market

## The Solution

Open-source collaboration to achieve:

- **87% cost reduction**
- **75% faster time to market**
- **100x scale** through community
- **85% global reach**
